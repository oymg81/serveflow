## Reflection

**1. What went well during this unit?**

During this unit, our team successfully defined the core structure of our project, including wireframes and the entity relationship diagram. We were able to clearly outline the main features and user flow of the application, which helped us better understand how the system should function. Collaboration between team members went well, and we maintained consistency between the frontend design and backend structure.

---

**2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?**

Designing the layout for the volunteer scheduling and event details views was challenging because the interface needed to be intuitive for both technical and non-technical users. Additionally, defining the structure of the many-to-many relationship using the `ASSIGNMENTS` join table between `USERS` and `EVENTS`, while incorporating a status field, required careful planning to ensure data integrity.

---

**3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?**

In upcoming units, we would benefit from additional guidance on implementing full-stack features, especially connecting the frontend and backend. Support with debugging, deployment, and best practices for structuring scalable applications would also be helpful as we continue developing our final project.
