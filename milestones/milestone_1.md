# Milestone 1 Reflection

1. **What is the name of your app?**
ServeFlow

2. **What is the problem your app solves?**
ServeFlow simplifies and centralizes volunteer scheduling for churches. It replaces the confusion of messaging apps and spreadsheets with a single organized platform.

3. **What is your overarching technical approach to solving it?**
We will build a full-stack PERN application (Postgres, Express, React, Node). The backend will handle relational logic between Users, Ministries, and Events. The frontend will provide a clean interface for admins to manage schedules and volunteers to submit availability.
