# ServeFlow Pitch Presentation (3 minutes)

**[0:00 - 0:30] Introduction & The Problem**
"Hi everyone, I'm Oscar and I'm here to introduce **ServeFlow**, a full-stack web application designed to help churches organize their volunteer scheduling.
Currently, many church coordinators are forced to manage hundreds of volunteers through chaotic group chats, verbal commitments, or messy spreadsheets. This leads to missing volunteers, double-booking, and a lot of unnecessary stress."

**[0:30 - 1:15] The Solution & Key Features**
"ServeFlow solves this by centralizing scheduling into one intuitive platform. 
Our core features include:
1. **Volunteer Selection**: Volunteers create an account, select their ministry (like Kids or Media), and request available service times that work for them.
2. **Admin Management**: Coordinators can create events, manage role assignments, and review volunteer availability in real-time. 
3. **Status Tracking**: Both admins and volunteers can see if an assignment is 'Pending' or 'Confirmed'."

**[1:15 - 2:00] Custom Features & Architecture**
"To elevate the experience, we're building two custom features:
First, **Advanced Filtering & Sorting**, allowing coordinators to quickly filter schedules by specific ministries or dates.
Second, **Dynamic Validation Logic** to prevent assigning volunteers to overlapping events or past dates, ensuring data integrity.
From a technical standpoint, we're building a PERN stack application with a robust Postgres relational database managing the many-to-many relationships between our users and events."

**[2:00 - 2:30] The Vision (Wireframes)**
"In our wireframes, you'll see a clean Admin Dashboard that gives a high-level calendar view of upcoming services. The Volunteer View is simplified and mobile-friendly, allowing users to easily mark their availability with just a click. We prioritized a fast and intuitive UI because volunteers need a tool that simplifies their contribution, not complicates it."

**[2:30 - 3:00] Conclusion**
"Ultimately, ServeFlow is about removing the friction from volunteering, so people can focus on serving their community instead of figuring out when they're supposed to show up. 
Thank you for listening, and we're excited to start development!"
